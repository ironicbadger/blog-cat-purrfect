PurrfectProse is Catherine's home for book reviews, reading challenges, audiobooks, and Reading with Ella notes.

The archive started in 2024 as a monthly reading project and has grown into a searchable collection of reviews by date, tag, format, author, and genre.

Posts are plain Markdown files, so new reviews can be written in VS Code, Obsidian, or any editor that can save a `.md` file with front matter.
