## <u>How to be a Little Sod by Simon Brett</u>

![](/images/ghost/2024/03/simon-brett.jpeg)

I'm ahead of the Monthly goals so I shoved a little extra light reading in this month (also for a break from Mr. Phinn). This book was funnier! This was loaned to me by my Mother-In-Law when I had Ella and again I never got round to reading it.

This follows the first year of a babies life, written from his perspective, as if his main goal in life is to make that of his parents incredibly difficult. He did a great job and even managed to finagle multiple A&E trips too. Well done Baby.

I'm so glad Ella is 3 now.

### Funny and lighthearted 5/10
