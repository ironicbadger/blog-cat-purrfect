What a fantastic audiobook! I read so many thrillers that are in fact - not thrilling. I am happy to announce this was not in that category. I was hooked from the very beginning. The story started at the end and slowly revealed the incident of the night before piece by piece, and I am happy to announce that I mislead several times on the journey.

Not only was the what-happened, who-dunnit part of the story fantastic, but the setting was evocative too. I could see the island in my minds-eye the entire time and it was spooky and fantastic.

Bonus points for the narrators too - top notch.

### Really thrilling 9/10
