## <u>Storm the Lightning Fairy (Weather Fairies #6)- by Daisy Meadows</u>

![](/images/ghost/2026/02/storm.jpeg)

Here's some interesting info: Daisy Meadows is not a person! My mental image of a bubbly blonde woman in her 20's or 30's writing sweet little stories for children is false! There are actually several people writing as Daisy Meadows; in this instance the author is actually named Linda Chapman. It does explain how there are so many of these books.

Miss Linda (I don't know why I'm calling her Miss Linda but it fits my new mental image) is one of the good ones - this was a particularly good weather fairies book. I enjoyed the culmination of the whole weather fairies epic!

#### The best Weather Fairies so far, I'll be looking out for more of Miss Linda 7/10
