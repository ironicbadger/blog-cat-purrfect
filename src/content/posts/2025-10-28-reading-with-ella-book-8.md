## <u>Rainbow Magic (#14), Weather Fairies (#7) Hayley the Rain Fairy by Daisy Meadows</u>

![](/images/ghost/2025/10/download-2.jpeg)

We found this in a bookbox without realizing that this is the seventh and final book in the weather fairies series. That's a bit of a pity as it was actually pretty good, and now we know how the series ends when we go back to read some other books.

It's fast paced, the human girl characters are likable and it's not glaringly badly written (seriously, that's all I ask for in a kids book). The fairy and goblin fight for good and evil respectively and unsurprisingly good wins! Yay.

We will keep our eyes out for other books in the series at the bookboxes and library.

#### Pretty decent 6/10
