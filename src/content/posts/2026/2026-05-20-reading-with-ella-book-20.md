## <u>Fantastic Mr. Fox by Roald Dahl</u>

![](/images/covers/2026/05/download--2-.webp)

Ahh! The GOAT of children's authors in my opinion. And this is one of this is one of his best books too. I loved sharing this book with Ella since I have strong memories of reading it myself, sat outside in the sunshine in Hethersett.

It's not too long, and its just a romp from start to finish. The villains are complete caricatures and Mr. Fox is everything a wily fox should be.

Ella loved it so much we read it through twice before I was allowed to move onto a new book.

#### An old favorite 9/10
