What a fantastic culmination of this trilogy. I was on the edge of my seat as the the Antari from the 3 different worlds worked together to defeat the darkness. Just wow.

I think book 2 was the best in terms of writing but the battle against evil in this book really hard my emotions in a tight fist all the way through.

Also Kell and Lila and Alucard and Rhy - chef's kiss, I love both couples. Yes, I know people still hate Lila, and honestly I see it more in this book.

The author has written more books in this world, but I'm ambivalent about whether it actually needed any more. I'm as yet undecided whether I will be reading them.

If I was a betting woman, I would say I expect a movie or TV adaption of these books sometime soon!

### Epic conclusion to a great trilogy 9/10
