## <u>Katie the Kitten Fairy (Rainbow Magic: Pet Keeper Fairies, #1)</u>

![](/images/covers/2026/05/download.webp)

Another of these fairy books, this one included a kitten. Who doesn't like a kitten? I've not bothered to look up which of the many writers wrote this one but it was adequate, although definitely not up to the level of the weather goblin one we read a little while ago.

Ella liked it well enough - but not as much as I expected, given her love of cats.

#### As far as these fairy books go, this was a middling one 6/10
