## <u>Amelia Bedelia Wraps it Up (Amelia Bedelia Special Holiday Book No1) by Herman Parish</u>

![](/images/covers/2026/04/shopping--1-.webp)

Christmas arrived here in September for reasons known best to a four year old. This is definitely one of the better Amelia Bedelia books out there, Amelia is her usual kind hearted but completely literal self. There are the usual Amelia Bedelia word based misunderstandings which I usually find just a bit irritating and Ella doesn't get at all. Oh well.

Ella did like the Christmas theme and all the kind things Amelia and her friends tried to do for their community. I appreciated the updated pictures and the attempt to minimize the amount of times I had to say the words "Amelia Bedelia". That gets so old!

#### One of the better Amelia's out there. A solid holiday read. 7/10
