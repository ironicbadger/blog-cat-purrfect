## <u>Paris Daillencourt Is About to Crumble by Alexis Hall</u>

![](/images/covers/2024/09/Paris.webp)

What a fun and cozy book. This is everything my pride month book wasn't. Set in the world of the Great British Bake Off (they call it bake expectations and have fictional judges and comedians but we know who they mean). Mary Berry is alive and well by a different name!

Could have done with a bit more romance and a little less of Paris having meltdowns but overall a very funny, very charming book.

### Who wouldn't like a book set in the Great British Bake Off universe 6/10
