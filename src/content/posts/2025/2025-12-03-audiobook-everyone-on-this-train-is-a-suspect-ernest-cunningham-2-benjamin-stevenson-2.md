You can probably guess since this is number 33 in the series, that I quite like Agatha Raisin. I liked this one too, even though it wasn't actually written by M.C. Beaton since M.C. Beaton is dead.

I gave that previous sentence my best M. C. Beaton abrupt styling - did it work? I'm not sure. Devil's Delight however - does work. It is written exactly as it would have been, had M.C. Beaton written it herself.

It's funny and clever and has nudists. I'm pretty sure M. C. would have wholeheartedly approved.

### I'm so happy Agatha lives on 7/10
