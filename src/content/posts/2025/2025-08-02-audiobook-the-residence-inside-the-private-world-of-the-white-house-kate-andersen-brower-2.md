I found the second book in this series in the bookbox and it looked interesting so I picked it up. When I found it was the second book I borrowed this, the first in the series, from libby.

Set in modern day America and featuring AI and CIA conspiracies, this feels on the money at the moment. Although this is a young adult book and therefore has the requisite amount of pining over boys and teenage drama, the meaty part of the story is really compelling and Mila is an appealing heroine.

### A great addition to the AI oeuvre 7/10
