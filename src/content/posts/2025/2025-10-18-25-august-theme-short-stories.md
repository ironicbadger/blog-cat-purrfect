## <u>Mavericks: Life stories and lessons of history's most extraordinary misfits by Jenny Draper</u>

![](/images/covers/2025/10/download.webp)

While only tentatively fitting the theme of short stories, since all the stories are of real people and there is a considerable number of sources cited at the back of the book, this was a fantastically fun read.

The writing was light-hearted and laugh-out-loud funny, the characters picked were vividly drawn and personable. I was impressed by the mix of men and women as well as people of colour and lgbtq representation. For a history book, it was refreshing not to be drowning in old white men. As an extra added bonus, the chapters were delightfully short too.

#### A fun little read which furnished me with many useless fun facts for every occasion. 8/10
