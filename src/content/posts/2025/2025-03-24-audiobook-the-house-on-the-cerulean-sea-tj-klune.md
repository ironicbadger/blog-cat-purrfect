Another fantastic fantasy book. I expected a book with the word cerulean in the title to either be amazing or amazingly pretentious. I am happy to report it is the first. The kids on the island were fantastical and adorable, and the audiobook narrator did a brilliant job with all their voices.

I found the love story between Linus and Arthur sweet and understated and completely believable.

### A lovely, feel good, gentle, cosy fantasy 9/10
