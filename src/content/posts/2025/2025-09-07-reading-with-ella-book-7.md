## <u>Dolphin School (#4) - Flip's Surprise Talent by Catherine Hapka</u>

![](/images/covers/2025/09/download-4.webp)

We're back with the dolphin friends again. I didn't think Ella especially liked the last book but she was up for trying another one. This was extremely similar to the last. Same dolphin kids, same structure just swap out shark with talent show. They call the talent show "show-off day" which I can't say I love.

Ella says I should give it five stars but I won't. It took about 2 weeks to read, this is not a long book, as Ella lost interest after about half a chapter of reading every day.

#### also un-problematic 2/10
