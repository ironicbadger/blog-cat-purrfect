## <u>Dolphin School (#3) - Splash's Secret Friend by Catherine Hapka</u>

![](/images/covers/2025/09/download.webp)

Well, our second chapter book was less inspiring. Ella liked it (it does have a pink cover) but honestly even for a kids book it wasn't well written. I kept thinking - I could do better than this. These thoughts popped in every second page or so.

The message of kindness and working together and being unprejudiced is all well and good though.

Apparently I am reading the next one soon. Yay.

#### un-problematic 2/10
