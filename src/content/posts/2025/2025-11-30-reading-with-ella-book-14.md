## <u>Sophie Mouse (#5) - The Maple Festival by Poppy Green</u>

![](/images/covers/2025/11/download--2-.webp)

This is our first Sophie Mouse book, and I thought it would be a winner since it's about baking. Ella and I are full into our autumn bake off baking kick right now, so a mouse with a bakery seemed a good plan.

It was fine, cute even and the illustrations were adorable. It was Beatrix Potter for the modern child, I think I might just be over cute talking woodland animals.

Ella wants to say - I loved the part where they sell the cakes

#### Lovely illustrations 6/10
