Heidi Swain cannot write male characters. And even her narrator's best efforts to give them different voices didn't make Jake, Ben, Tom and Giles any less interchangeable. Our poor protagonist had a tough choice with that sea of meh. I was half hoping for a surprise twist where where she ended up with a woman but no such luck, she ends up with the high school crush as we all expected from about page 5.

Other than the crappy male love interests, the cafe itself was cute and I liked little Ella. But I might be biased there.

### Wouldn't bother if I was you, but at least it was inoffensive. 3/10
