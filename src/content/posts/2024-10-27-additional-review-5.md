## <u>The No-Show by Beth O'Leary</u>

![](/images/ghost/2024/10/noshow.jpeg)

Thanks Caitlin for the loan of this surprisingly good book. I thought it would be a typical rom-com chick lit kinda deal. It was a bit more complicated than that.

Nicely written and easy to read, it felt reminiscent of Marian Keyes with the Dublin angle. The story fitted together well and I didn't guess the twist for quite a while.

### Pretty entertaining. 6/10
