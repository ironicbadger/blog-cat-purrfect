## <u>Magic Treehouse (#5) - Night of the Ninjas by Mary Pope Osborne</u>

![](/images/ghost/2025/11/download-2.jpeg)

This Magic Treehouse book was actually a little scary for Ella. Although the Ninjas turn out to be the good guys, the fact that they don't talk, wear masks and the intimidating front cover were too much for my bug.

Ella wants to say - I didn't particularly like this one but I do love the other Jack and Annie books.

These Magic Tree House book are generally a hit, so I've bought a bunch for Ella for her Birthday!

#### Not the best of the Magic Tree Houses. Too scary! 6/10
